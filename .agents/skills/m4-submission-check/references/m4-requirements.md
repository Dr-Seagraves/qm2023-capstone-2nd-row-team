# M4 Requirements Summary

Source: `docs/README (m4.pdf`

## Core Goal

Milestone 4 is a professional memo for a non-technical decision-maker. The reader should be able to understand the findings and make an informed decision without reading code or regression output.

## Team Memo Deliverable

- File: `Final_Investment_Memo.pdf`
- Format: professional PDF
- Length: 5-7 pages
- Required sections:
  - Executive Summary
  - Methodology
  - Results
  - Conclusions and Recommendations
  - References
  - Appendix: AI Audit

## Team Memo Section Targets

### Executive Summary

- About 0.5 page
- 2-3 sentences stating the key finding
- 1-2 sentences with a specific recommendation
- No tables or figures

### Methodology

- About 1 page
- Data sources with citations
- Sample construction: counts and date range
- Model specifications
- Variable definitions

### Results

- About 1.5-2 pages
- Table 1: main model
- Table 2: alternative specification
- Figure 1: key visualization
- Figure 2: diagnostic plot
- Interpretation prose that translates coefficients into economic magnitude

### Conclusions and Recommendations

- About 1 page
- Actionable recommendation
- Risk assessment
- Caveats and limitations

### References

- About 0.5 page
- Data sources with URLs
- Any academic references cited
- APA or similar format

### Appendix: AI Audit

- About 0.5-1 page
- Summary of AI use across milestones
- Verification and critique examples

## Individual Addendum Deliverable

- File: `Individual_Addendum_[YourName].pdf`
- Format: PDF
- Length: exactly 1 page
- Required content:
  - Personal contribution with specific tasks and hours
  - One defended methodological decision with evidence
  - One key limitation and why it matters
  - AI audit notes if needed

## Rubric Summary

- Reproducibility and rigor: repo runs end-to-end and memo values match code outputs
- Structure and clarity: organized, professional, jargon-free
- Results and interpretation: publication-ready visuals and clear economic interpretation
- Recommendations and caveats: actionable advice with honest limitations
- Individual addendum: specific contribution, defended decision, key limitation, honesty

## Common Pitfalls

- Executive summary is too technical
- No recommendation is stated
- Limitations are ignored
- Individual contribution is vague
- Raw regression output is pasted without formatting

## Final Checklist

- All required sections present
- Team memo length is 5-7 pages
- Individual addendum is exactly 1 page
- Tables are formatted
- Figures are high resolution
- No unexplained jargon or acronyms
- Recommendation is specific
- Caveats are honest
- Names appear on both deliverables
- Files are submitted as PDFs
- AI audit appendix is included in the team memo
